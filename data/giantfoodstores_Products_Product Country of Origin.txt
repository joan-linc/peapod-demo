Certain products must comply with country of origin labeling (COOL) requirements. Those products are beef, lamb, pork, fish, perishable agricultural commodities such as fruits and vegetables, and peanuts, ginseng, pecans and macadamia nuts. To determine the country of origin of these products, view the item detail. If there are multiple countries of origin possible for a certain product, then every possible country will be listed in the item detail. Upon receipt of the item, most packaging will reflect the specific country.
