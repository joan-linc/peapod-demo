Quantity Limits

We reserve the right to limit your order or the quantity of a particular product that you may order. Although our goal is to fulfill 100% of your order, item availability for large quantities is limited and we do not back-order items.

 We request you contact our Customer Care Department at 1-888-814-4268 at least one week in advance should your order include quantity amounts greater than 20. Additional handling fees may apply for orders containing large sizes and/or weights.

  Rain Checks

If a sale item you ordered is unavailable, please contact Customer Care at 1-888-814-4268 and they will issue you an "electronic rain check."
