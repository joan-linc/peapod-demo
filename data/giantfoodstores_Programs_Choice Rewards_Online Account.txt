An online account is required to take advantage of the new redemption options - Grocery Dollars & Free stuff! Having an online account unlocks additional benefits including digital coupons, Deli Order Ahead, shopping list and now even more with Choice Rewards benefits. An online account is NOT required to redeem points for gas discounts.

Customers can sign-up for an online account using the mobile app or website.  A card or Alt ID is required for existing GIANT card members to link the new online account to their card. Customers without a card can also request a card online when creating an online account.

Customers who do not have the ability to create an online account can continue to use their card for everyday savings on Bonus Buys and discounts on gas with every 100 points.
