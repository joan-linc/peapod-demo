Once you created your GIANT Direct for Business account, you can send the following information to us at b2b.team@peapoddigitallabs.com.

Account Email
First and Last Name
Company Name
Shipping Address
FEIN or TIN
Attach a copy of the PA Tax Exempt Certificate

Keep in mind that you need to do this prior to placing an order. If you have placed any orders in the past, the tax exemption will not apply retroactively to your previous orders.
